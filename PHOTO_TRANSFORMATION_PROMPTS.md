## 1. Old Painting (Rembrandt Style)

```
Edit this photo to transform it into a classical oil painting from the 1700s in the style of Rembrandt.

Style Requirements:
- Rich, dark background with dramatic lighting (chiaroscuro effect)
- Warm, earthy color palette: deep browns, golden yellows, dark reds
- Visible brush strokes and oil painting texture
- Renaissance-era clothing: elaborate collars, rich fabrics, period-appropriate attire
- Formal, dignified pose as in classical portraiture
- Subtle aging effects: slight cracks in paint, antique canvas texture

Important:
- Transform the person into a noble or merchant from the 1700s
- Background should be a dark, atmospheric interior with subtle details
- Maintain the person's recognizable features while adding classical painting qualities
- Child-friendly and historically inspired
```

- Note which prompts work well and which need refinement
- Pay attention to:
  - Does it maintain the person's recognizability?
  - Is the transformation child-friendly?

  - Are the colors vibrant and appealing?
  - Is the background properly transformed?
